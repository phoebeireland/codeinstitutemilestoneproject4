About Us & Our Team

About Us

First established in 1987, Evergreen Funeral Home has been the number one funeral care service in town for the last 20 years. Our motto is: "Death isn't pretty, but your funeral can be!", and all of our staff members take this to heart. We know that the death of a loved one can be a tough time for most families, so we do our best to alleviate the stress of planning a funeral.

Our Team

At Evergreen Funeral Home, our team of dedicated staff will work to make your funeral the best funeral it can be. Our Funeral Director, John Bingham, has been employed by Evergreen for over ten years, and leads a small staff of four.
Recently, we hired top funeral accessory designer, Madison Keeley, to design our own exclusive line of funeral products. Her latest designs can be found at our online shop. Encase your dearly departed in one of her newest designs today.
